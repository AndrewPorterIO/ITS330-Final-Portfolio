Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina

Situated in the Greater Sarajevo Valley and surrounded by the Dinaric Alps mountain range, Sarajevo is the largest city and capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina. This city is famous for being the location of the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand, an event that sparked the onset of World War I.

Come to this city and see the wonderful mixture of Catholic cathedrals, Muslim mosques, and Jewish synagogues. Experience the wonderful flavors of Balkan cuisine, and visit the Olympic Park that was built for the 1984 Olympic Games. Walk through a wonderful old town full of shops, plazas, and cafes. Stop in at one of the cafes and try some authentic Bosnian-style coffee!
